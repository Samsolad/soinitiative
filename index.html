Programme 01  ·  Community Food Support  ·  All of Africa

So Family

A community-funded programme that surprises families in need across Africa with a full meal — delivered with care, filmed in the moment, shared so the next family gets nominated too.

What is So Family?

A meal. A moment.
A movement.

So Family is a community-powered food programme where anyone from anywhere nominates a family in Africa that needs support. A local restaurant partner prepares a full family meal. A verified local coordinator delivers it unannounced. The genuine reaction is filmed and shared.

This is not charity. It is the community deciding who deserves care — and acting on it. Every delivery proves ordinary people can create extraordinary moments.

No government money. No NGO gatekeepers. Just people, giving back.

How It Works

Five steps.
One family. Real impact.

01

You Nominate

Fill in the form below. Tell us who the family is, where they are in Africa, and why they deserve this. You don't need to know them personally.

02

We Verify

A trusted local coordinator confirms the nomination within 48 hours. The family's privacy is fully protected throughout.

03

We Deliver

A local restaurant partner prepares a full family meal. Our coordinator delivers it directly to their home. The family doesn't know it's coming.

04

We Film

The moment is filmed naturally, respectfully, and with full consent. No scripts. No staging. A real family, receiving real care.

05

We Share — And You Share

The story goes live on YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok. Download the e-flier below and share it. Every share brings us closer to the next family.

So Business

A community spotlight that finds a small African business with a great product but no marketing budget — sets them up free, films their journey, and shares it so the whole continent can see them.

What is So Business?

Great product.
No budget. Not for long.

So Business is a community spotlight where anyone nominates an African small business they believe in — great product, no marketing budget. We onboard them free, recruit their first Brand Ambassadors, and film the moment their first sale arrives.

This is not advertising. It is the community investing in African businesses. Every episode proves community commerce works.

No application fee. No paid placement. No gatekeepers. The community nominates. We deliver the spotlight.

Five steps.
One business. A real first sale.

01

You Nominate

Programme 03  ·  Wedding Sponsorship  ·  All of Africa

So Wedding

Nominate a couple in Africa who deserve their dream wedding but cannot afford it. The community nominates. The community votes. The winner receives up to ₦1,000,000 towards their wedding day.

What is So Wedding?

Love deserves
a proper celebration.

So Wedding is a community-powered wedding sponsorship programme. Anyone can nominate a couple in Africa who are deeply in love, ready to commit, but don't have the budget for the wedding they deserve. The community then votes on who should receive the sponsorship.

The winning couple receives up to ₦1,000,000 towards their wedding — funded entirely by community contributions and commercial sponsors. Every stage is filmed, from nomination discovery to the wedding day itself.

This is not a TV show. It is not a competition for entertainment. It is the community choosing to honour love — and filming the proof that it happened.

How It Works

Five steps.
One couple. One unforgettable day.

01

You Nominate

Fill in the nomination form below. Tell us about the couple — their story, where they are in Africa, and why they deserve this.

02

We Verify

Our team verifies each nomination within 72 hours. Verified couples are added to the public voting page with their consent.

03

The Community Votes

Voting is open to everyone. One vote per person per couple. The voting period runs for two weeks. The couple with the most verified votes at close wins the sponsorship.

04

We Plan and Fund the Wedding

The winning couple receives up to ₦1,000,000 towards their wedding, planned with our local partners. Every supplier is coordinated by SoInitiative on their behalf.

05

We Film the Day — And Share It

The entire journey is filmed — from nomination to vows. The wedding day video goes live across YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok.

Programme 04  ·  Monthly Recognition Award  ·  Africa

So Hero

Nominate the worker or campus leader in Africa who truly deserves recognition. Colleagues and students vote. The highest votes win a ₦200,000 monthly cash gift — one workplace winner, one campus winner, every month.

What is So Hero?

Every organisation has
someone who holds it together.

So Hero is a monthly recognition award for the people who show up, lead quietly, and make their teams and communities better — without ever being celebrated for it. Anyone can nominate a deserving person in their workplace or on their campus across Africa.

There are two separate categories every month. The Workplace Award recognises an employee, team leader, manager, or any staff member in any organisation. The Campus Award recognises a student representative, student union leader, course rep, or lecturer at any tertiary institution across Africa.

The community votes. The highest votes in each category wins ₦200,000 — deposited directly to the winner. No panel. No management approval. Just the community deciding who deserves to be seen.

How It Works

Five steps.
One hero. ₦200,000.

01

You Nominate

Fill in the form below. Choose whether the nomination is for the Workplace category or the Campus category. Tell us who the person is, what organisation or institution they belong to, and why they deserve this.

02

We Verify

Our team verifies each nomination within 48 hours. Verified nominees are added to the public voting board under the correct category. Their story is shared with their consent.

03

The Community Votes

Voting is open to everyone. Colleagues, classmates, and the public all vote. One vote per person per nominee. Voting closes at the end of each calendar month. One winner per category.

04

₦200,000 Is Paid to the Winner

The winner in each category receives ₦200,000 deposited directly to their account. The award is announced publicly. The moment is filmed and shared — with full consent.

05

The Story Goes Live — And a New Cycle Begins

The winner's story is published across YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and LinkedIn. Nominations open for the next month immediately. Download the e-flier and share it to bring in the next hero.
